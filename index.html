Welcome To Hi Theme

Hi Classic Theme is a lightweight and minimalist WordPress theme designed for those who appreciate simplicity and flexibility. It is fully compatible with Elementor, allowing you to easily customize your website’s design. With Hi Classic, you can personalize your header and footer without needing the pro version of Elementor, making it an excellent choice for users who want a clean and customizable website without the bloat.

Hi Widgets

The Hi Widgets are specially designed for Elementor and provide additional features to enhance your website’s functionality. These widgets help you display dynamic content, templates, sliders, and more.

Hi Dynamic Content

The Hi Dynamic Content widget is a powerful tool that allows you to display all your posts or products dynamically. With customizable settings, you can control the layout, the number of rows, and the number of posts or products displayed per page.

Hi Dynamic Post

The Hi Dynamic Post widget is designed specifically for displaying blog posts. With this widget, you have full control over which categories of posts to showcase, along with customizable layout options.

Template Display

The Hi Template Display widget allows you to showcase any saved Elementor templates on your pages with ease. This feature enables you to select and display pre-designed templates within the Page or Container tab, giving you full control over your website’s layout and design.

Hi Slider

The Hi Slider widget is a user-friendly and customizable slider that allows you to showcase products, content, or banners effortlessly. Whether you're highlighting featured items, blog posts, or promotional banners, this slider offers full control over layout and styling.

Custom CSS

The Custom CSS feature allows you to apply page-specific styling without affecting the entire website. You can add custom CSS at the bottom of the page editor, ensuring precise design control. Please note that this CSS will not be visible in the Elementor front-end builder but will be applied when the page is live. This feature is fully compatible with both the Classic Editor and Gutenberg Editor.
